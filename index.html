About Me

I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Communication at Stanford University, with a minor in Political Science. I'm advised by Jennifer Pan and a Knight-Hennessy Scholar.

Before academia, I worked as a journalist based in Beijing, reporting for the Los Angeles Times, Sixth Tone, and other outlets. I hold an M.A. in China Studies from Peking University's Yenching Academy and a B.A. from Amherst College.

Research Areas

  • Foreign Correspondence, Journalism Studies, Media-State Relations
  • Digital Propaganda, Political Communication, Censorship
  • Computational Social Science, Text Analysis, Mixed Methods
